# Managing Categories & Sub-Categories

Structure:
- **Category:** Broad group (Medicine, FMCG, Surgical, Equipment).
- **Sub-Category:** Specific type (Tablet, Syrup, Injection, Skincare).

Setup:
- Inventory → Settings → Categories.
- Click "Add Category".

Benefit:
- Sales Reports allow filtering by category (e.g., "Show me antibiotic sales").
