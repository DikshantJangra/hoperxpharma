# Storage Conditions & Cold Chain

Setup:
- Product Master → Storage Field.
- Options: Room Temp, Cool (8-25°C), Cold (2-8°C), Frozen.

Alerts:
- "Cold" items show a snowflake icon in POS and Receiving.
- Reminder to "Put in Fridge" appears upon receiving stock.
