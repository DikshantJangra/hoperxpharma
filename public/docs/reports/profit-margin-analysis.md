# Profit Margin Analysis

**Formula:**
`Margin % = ((Selling Price - Cost Price) / Selling Price) * 100`

**Report Insights:**
- **High Volume, Low Margin:** Fast-moving consumer goods (FMCG).
- **Low Volume, High Margin:** Specialized surgical equipment.
- **Negative Margin:** Items sold below cost (loss leaders or errors).
