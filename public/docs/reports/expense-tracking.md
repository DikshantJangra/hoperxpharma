# Expense Tracking

**Logging:**
- Finance → Expenses → Add Expense.
- Categories: Rent, Utilities, Salary, Maintenance, Marketing.

**Analysis:**
- View "Expense vs Revenue" chart.
- Identify cost-saving opportunities (e.g., high electricity bill → switch to LED).
