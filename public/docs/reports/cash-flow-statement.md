# Cash Flow Statement

**Inflow:**
- Cash Sales.
- Customer Credit Payments.

**Outflow:**
- Supplier Payments.
- Operational Expenses (Rent, Salaries).

**Net Cash:**
- Daily/Monthly view of liquidity.
- Helps plan large purchases.
