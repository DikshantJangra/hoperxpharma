# GST Reconciliation Report

**Purpose:**
- Ensure you claim the correct Input Tax Credit (ITC).
- Identify suppliers who haven't filed their returns.

**Workflow:**
1. Download GSTR-2B JSON from GST Portal.
2. Upload to HopeRx Reports → GST Recon.
3. System matches invoices by GSTIN, Invoice No, and Date.
4. **Mismatches:** Highlighted in red (e.g., Supplier filed ₹1000, you recorded ₹1200).
