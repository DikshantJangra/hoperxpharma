# Inventory Turnover Ratio

**Formula:**
`Turnover = Cost of Goods Sold (COGS) / Average Inventory Value`

**Interpretation:**
- **High Ratio:** Efficient! You sell stock quickly (fresh cash flow).
- **Low Ratio:** Overstocking. Cash is tied up in dead stock.

**Target:**
- Pharmacy industry average is 10-12 times per year.
