# Customer Retention Rate

**Metric:**
- **Repeat Customer Rate:** % of customers who made >1 purchase in the selected period.
- **Churn Rate:** % of customers who haven't visited in >90 days.

**Action:**
- Send "We Miss You" SMS coupons to churned customers.
- Enroll high-value repeat customers in Loyalty Program.

Related: `/docs/customers/loyalty-program`
