# Disposal of Expired Medicines

**Non-Returnable Items:**
- Broken vials, loose tablets, or items supplier won't take back.

**Process:**
1. Inventory → Expired Stock → Select Items → "Mark for Disposal".
2. Generate "Destruction Certificate".
3. Hand over to authorized bio-medical waste collector.
4. Attach waste collection receipt to the certificate.
