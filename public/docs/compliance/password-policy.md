# Password Policy & Security

**Requirements:**
- Minimum 8 characters.
- Must change every 90 days (configurable).
- Account locks after 5 failed attempts.

**Best Practices:**
- Do not share passwords.
- Each staff member must have their own login.
- Use "Switch User" feature instead of sharing a logged-in screen.

Related: `/docs/settings/users-roles`
