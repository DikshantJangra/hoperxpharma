# Credit Notes & Adjustments

**Credit Note:** Issued when a customer returns goods or there was an overcharge.
- Linked to original invoice.
- Reduces tax liability for that month.

**Debit Note:** Issued to supplier for returns or undercharge.

Creating:
- Billing → Credit Notes → New.
- Select Customer and Original Invoice.
- Enter items/amount.
