# Supplier Performance & Ratings

The system automatically rates suppliers (1-5 stars) based on:
- **Fill Rate:** Did they deliver 100% of ordered items?
- **Timeliness:** Did they deliver within the promised lead time?
- **Price Variance:** Did invoice price match PO price?
