# Refund a Sale

Quick Steps
1. Billing → Transactions.
2. Search by receipt or customer.
3. Click **Refund** → choose items → Confirm.
4. Select refund method (cash/card/UPI).

Notes
- Card refunds may require a reversal via the card terminal — follow your PG instructions.
- Partial refunds supported.

Troubleshooting:
- If transaction not found, search by date range or contact support to check sync logs.
