# e-Prescriptions & Digital Rx

**Sources:**
- **Direct Doctor Portal:** Doctors using HopeRx Clinic app can send Rx directly.
- **ABDM (India):** Scan patient's ABHA QR code to fetch digital records (requires consent).
- **Telehealth Integrations:** Practo, 1mg, etc. (via API).

**Workflow:**
1. Notification appears in "New Rx" queue.
2. Click to open.
3. Medicines are auto-mapped to inventory.
4. Click "Convert to Sale".
