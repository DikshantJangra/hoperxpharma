# Clinical Alerts & Interactions

**Severity Levels:**
1. **Contraindicated (Red):** Do not dispense. High risk of death/injury. (e.g., Viagra + Nitrates).
2. **Major (Orange):** Monitor closely or adjust dose.
3. **Minor (Yellow):** Inform patient (e.g., decreased absorption).

**Allergy Alerts:**
- Checks drug against patient's "Allergies" list in profile.
- e.g., Penicillin allergy → Amoxicillin Rx → **STOP ALERT**.
